About AnnaLilis

AnnaLilis is a fashion brand in Taiwan, founded by YC, Nai, YY, NY in Taoyuan Zhongli in 2020. AnnaLilis has become one of the top brands in the field of jewelry and rings, and now the brand of AnnaLilis is not limited to designing and selling high-end accessories, but has become involved in fashion, jewelry, watches, cosmetics, perfume and other fields.The two diamond ring logos with its brand name are well-known as luxury products. Its headquarters are located on the 88th floor of the Taipei 101 building.

The style of AnnaLilis is gorgeous and bright, presenting the inspiration and design essence of it's jewelry designers YC, Nai, YY and NY. It's personality allows AnnaLilis to freely express unlimited creativity, and has accumulated superb craftsmanship and deep tradition over the years. And the promise, AnnaLilis , which inherited and made outstanding achievements

Our vision lies in sustainable development, maintaining a perfect price-performance ratio of the product, so that the VIPs who buy AnnaLilis jewelry can bring them unlimited joy and satisfaction. AnnaLilis establishes a balance between parity and luxury, so that the public can more easily follow AnnaLilis fashion pace and walk in the forefront of trends; However, in recent years, the awareness of environmental protection has risen, and AnnaLilis is moving towards a method that uses environmentally friendly materials to make products in order to maintain our good and beautiful environment. AnnaLilis uses part of the profit of the year to help people in need of help in the society, and establishes the AnnaLilis Social Service Foundation to support these charity activities.
